## Art & audio

All in-repo visuals are generated from Godot primitive meshes and procedural
materials (no third-party art is bundled). Audio files are not bundled; the game
degrades gracefully to silence until you add your own.

When you add third-party assets, record them here with their license, e.g.:

| Asset | Author | Source | License |
|---|---|---|---|
| _example_coin.wav_ | _Author_ | _URL_ | _CC0 / CC-BY / OFL / ..._ |

## Fonts

The project uses Godot's default font. If you bundle a custom font, add its
license here (many game fonts are OFL or CC0).
